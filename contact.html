We're particularly interested in hearing about:

  • Questions: Theological, philosophical, or practical questions about the material

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Dragon Bible a real religious text?

No, the Dragon Bible is a work of creative fiction and mythological reinterpretation. It's not intended as religious doctrine or historical fact, but as an artistic exploration of spiritual themes.

Do you offer physical books?

Currently, the Dragon Bible is only available online. We're exploring options for physical publications and will announce any developments through our blog.

Can I use Dragon Bible content in my own work?

Please contact us for permission before using any Dragon Bible content beyond brief quotes for review or commentary purposes. We're generally supportive of creative responses to the work.

Are there study groups or communities?

While we don't officially organize groups, many readers have formed their own discussion circles. If you're interested in connecting with other readers, let us know and we may be able to facilitate introductions.

I'm experiencing intense reactions to the texts. Is this normal?

Some readers report strong emotional, physical, or spiritual responses. While this can be part of engaging with mythological material, please maintain grounding practices and seek appropriate support if experiences become overwhelming.

Media Inquiries

For press inquiries, interview requests, or media kit information, please email with "Media Inquiry" in the subject line. We're happy to discuss the creative process, mythological themes, and the role of modern mythology in spiritual exploration.
