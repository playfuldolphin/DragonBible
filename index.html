About The Dragon Bible

The Dragon Bible is a profound reimagining of sacred texts through the lens of an ancient truth: that humanity and dragonkind were once unified as a single, glorious being.

  • The Book of Enoch, revealing the true nature of the Watchers
  • Gnostic texts including the Nag Hammadi library
  • Original interpretations exploring the mystical bond between species
