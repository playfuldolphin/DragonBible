Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 8, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Dragon Bible ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website dragonbible.com (the "Site"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Comments: When you post comments on our Site, we collect your name (or pseudonym) and comment content.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and access times.
  • Cookies: We and our third-party partners use cookies and similar tracking technologies.
  • Local Storage: Comments and reactions are stored locally in your browser.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our Site, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

3.2 Google Fonts

Our Site uses Google Fonts. When you visit our Site, your browser downloads the required fonts directly from Google servers. This may involve transmitting your IP address to Google.

4. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Site
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze Site usage and trends
  • Respond to comments and inquiries

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function properly
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Site

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our Site.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt-out of Personalized Ads: Visit Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Browser Controls: Most browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences
  • Do Not Track: We currently do not respond to Do Not Track signals

7. Data Retention

Comments and reactions stored in your browser's local storage remain until you clear your browser data. We do not store this information on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

11. International Users

If you access our Site from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

14. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

15. GDPR Rights (For EU Residents)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data.
